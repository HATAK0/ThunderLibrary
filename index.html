DOOM

DOOM (2016) - is a fast-paced first-person shooter set on Mars. Experiments have opened a portal to Hell. Space trooper fights with demons, the goal is to close the portal. Brutality and modern graphics.

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt - is a role-playing game in the fantasy world. Heralt from Rivia searches for the missing Ciri, fights with monsters. Fascinating plot, open world, complex moral choices.

Cyberpunk 2077

Cyberpunk 2077 - is an RPG set in a dystopian future world. V, a mercenary, searches for a path to immortality in Knight City. Technology, conspiracies, personalization. Choices affect the outcome of the story.

Dying Light

Dying Light - is an action horror game set in a zombie apocalypse. The protagonist, Kyle Crane, survives in the city of Harran. Parkour, melee combat, day-night dynamics. Rescue the inhabitants and solve the mystery of the infection.
